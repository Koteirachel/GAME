Introduction

Pet lyfe is a game that was made to spread awareness on pet abuse. It is meant to teach kids the best way to take care of a pet.
Definition of pet abuse
What is considered as pet abuse? according to https://safevoices.org/ "Animal abuse, or animal cruelty is the crime of inflicting physical pain, suffering or death on an animal, usually a tame one, beyond necessity for normal discipline. '' . Any scenario where a pet is not being treated with the absolute most care can be considered pet abuse which is why it is crucial to know the essentials to taking care of a pet are explained in our game “Pet Lyfe”.
